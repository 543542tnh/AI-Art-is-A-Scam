My Opinions

According to my observation, the intention behind of using an AI art generator is the issue. As I mentioned before, some people use AI art generators for entertainment which is not a problem as people are allowed to have enjoyment and likes to play mindless entertainment. Since it is similar to the customisable icon. However, many people use AI art to deceive their clients, believing they will receive an original artwork created by them, not an artwork generated through AI art which they could have done themselves for free. Furthermore, more often or not it is a stolen art as AI art generator ‘borrowed’ from original artists’ artworks.

In my opinion, there should be a restriction on the use of AI. I agree AI is very useful as it is efficient and less time-consuming making the workplace much easier and accessible for anyone. However, it does not mean that AI should be placed in every industry especially in creative industries as there is a limit to what AI can do as evidenced by the cases I have shown.

The main purpose of AI should be to provide aid and lessen the workload for humans to work efficiently and effectively, not providing potential scams and increased stress and anxiety for people in creative industries who are potentially out of work due to AI overtaking their jobs.
